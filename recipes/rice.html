Perfect Rice

Ingredients

  • Your grain choice of rice! (i.e. Brown rice, short, medium, or long grain)
  • Rice Cooker (Any brand you are comfortable with. I use tiger or zojirushi brand!)
  • 3 cups water

Steps

  1. With your choice of rice, measure out 3 cups of uncooked rice grain and pour into bowl provided with the rice cooker.
  2. Wash the uncooked rice grain under lukewarm or cold water and drain as much excess water without spilling rice.
  3. Repeat step 2 until rice water in bowl has become clear.
  4. Drain as much excess water as possible.
  5. Measure out 3 and 1/4 cups of water and pour into rice bowl.
  6. Place rice bowl into rice cooker.
  7. Make sure your rice cooker is plugged into a power outlet!
  8. Now switch rice cooker from "keep warm" setting, to "cook" setting.
  9. Now let the magic happen! Within 15-30 minutes, your rice will be cooked!
  10. Pro Tip: After rice is cooked, allow rice to sit for at least 5-10 minutes in rice cooker before serving.
