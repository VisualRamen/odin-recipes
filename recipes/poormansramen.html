Poor Man's Ramen

Ingredients

  • Maruchan brand noodles
  • Your choice of inexpensive protein (Spam, vienna sausage, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup Canola oil or Vegetable oil
  • 2 and a half to 3 cups Water
  • Boiled egg (optional)
  • Sriracha (optional)
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Boil 2 and half to 3 cups water in a pot on high heat.
  2. Once water is boiled, set stovetop to medium-high heat and place uncooked noodles into pot.
  3. Cook until noodles are desired firmness. (soft, medium, or hard.)
  4. Place cooked noodles into a bowl.
  5. Cut choice of protein into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Pour 1/2 cup of oil into nonstick pan.
  7. Pour cut protein into pan.
  8. Fry until desired doneness.
  9. Place cut pieces into bowl of ramen.
  10. Optional: Cut green onions and garnish over ramen bowl.
  11. Optional: Place boiled egg into ramen bowl.
  12. Optional: Drizzle ramen bowl with Sriracha.
  13. SERVE.
