Spam Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 can Spam
  • 3 cups cooked short grain rice
  • 1/2 cup Sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup Canola or Vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 green onion

Steps

  1. Place wok on stovetop and set to high heat.
  2. Dice spam into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Pour canola/vegetable oil into wok.
  4. Pour spam into wok and set to medium-high heat.
  5. Fry spam until dark pink.
  6. Pour 3 cups of cooked rice into wok and mix well with spam.
  7. Pour 1 cup soy sauce and 1/2 tablespoon fish sauce and mix well.
  8. Crack 2 eggs and pour into wok and mix until egg is cooked.
  9. Add 1 tablespoon of pepper and 1/2 cup sesame oil to fried rice and continue mixing.
  10. Turn off heat.
  11. Cut 1 green onion and garnish over fried rice.
  12. TIME FO GRIND!
